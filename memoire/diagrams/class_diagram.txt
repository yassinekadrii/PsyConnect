Relationships:
- A User can be a Patient, a Psychologist, or an Admin (Inheritance).
- A Patient can book many Appointments (1 to *).
- A Psychologist can have many Appointments (1 to *).
- An Appointment leads to exactly one Session (1 to 1).
- A Psychologist can write many Prescriptions for Patients (1 to *).
- A Patient has many Prescriptions issued to them (1 to *).
- A Patient makes a Payment for an Appointment (1 to 1).
- Users (Patients and Psychologists) can send and receive many Messages (* to *).
